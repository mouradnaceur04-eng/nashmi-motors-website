Who We Are

Nashmi Motors — Harrisburg's Independent Used Car Dealer

Nashmi Motors is an independent used car dealership located at 8001 Paxton Street in Harrisburg, PA. We carry a hand-selected inventory of vehicles — sedans, SUVs, trucks, and vans — chosen to cover a range of budgets and needs.

Our approach is straightforward: clearly priced vehicles, no-pressure sales, and simple financing on every vehicle. We want the buying process to be easy and stress-free.

We also buy vehicles outright and accept trade-ins, so if you have a car you're looking to move, come talk to us.
